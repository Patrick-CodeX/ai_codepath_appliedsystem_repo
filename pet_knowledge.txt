PET CARE GUIDELINES:
1. Toxic Foods: Grapes, raisins, onions, garlic, and chocolate are highly toxic to dogs and cats.
2. Exercise: Dogs generally need 30-60 minutes of exercise daily.
3. Hydration: Cats often prefer running water or wet food to stay hydrated.
4. Emergencies: If a pet is lethargic or refuses food for more than 24 hours, contact a vet.
